Vision & Mission

VISION

To foster innovation and technical excellence by exploring the world of drones, empowering students to design, build, and implement UAV technologies that create real-world impact.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide a collaborative platform for learning, researching, and innovating in the field of UAVs, encouraging creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving to address societal and industrial needs.
